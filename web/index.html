How it works

Layer 1
Self-referencing baseline
EWMA over 8-12 weeks of (model, day, hour) data. Detects anomalies against each model's own history. Downdetector method.
Layer 2
Cross-model disproportionality
FDA Proportional Reporting Ratio adapted for AI complaints. Compares each model's complaint share against its historical share. PRR method.
Signal
Pure rage
One input: /fuck. No ratings, no surveys. The crowd's frustration IS the data. Deduplicated by IP per model per hour.
